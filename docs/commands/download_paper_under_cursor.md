doc_body:
Download the paper under mouse cursor. This can be the actual name of the paper (either in the document itself or the overview window) or a reference to the paper (e.g. executing @command(download_paper_under_cursor) while cursor is on a reference like `[12]` will download the paper referenced by `[12]`).
